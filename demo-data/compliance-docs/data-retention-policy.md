# Meridian Insurance Group — Data Retention Policy

## Retention Schedule

| Data Category | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Active policy records | Duration of policy + 7 years | Insurance regulation, limitation periods |
| Lapsed policy records | 7 years from lapse date | Limitation periods |
| Claims records | 7 years from settlement/closure | Insurance regulation, Limitation Act 1980 |
| Bodily injury claims | 21 years from settlement | Extended limitation period |
| Underwriting data | Duration of policy + 7 years | Risk assessment requirements |
| Payment records | 7 years from transaction | Financial regulation, HMRC requirements |
| Customer communications | 3 years from date of communication | FCA conduct rules |
| Portal access logs | 2 years | Security monitoring |
| Marketing consent records | Until withdrawal + 1 year | GDPR accountability |
| Complaint records | 5 years from resolution | FCA complaint handling rules |

## Destruction

Data past its retention period is securely destroyed within 90 days. Electronic data is overwritten or cryptographically erased. Physical documents are cross-cut shredded by approved contractors.

## Exceptions

Retention periods may be extended where data is subject to a legal hold, regulatory investigation, or ongoing litigation.
