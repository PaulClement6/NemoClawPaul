# Meridian Insurance Group — GDPR Data Processing Summary

## Lawful Basis for Processing

Meridian processes personal data under the following lawful bases (GDPR Article 6):

- **Contract performance** (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to administer your insurance policy, assess claims, and manage renewals.
- **Legal obligation** (Article 6(1)(c)): Processing required by insurance regulation, anti-money laundering legislation, and financial services conduct rules.
- **Legitimate interests** (Article 6(1)(f)): Fraud prevention, risk assessment, and service improvement. We conduct balancing tests to ensure your interests are not overridden.

## Data We Collect

- Identity data: Name, date of birth, address, contact details
- Financial data: Payment information, billing history, claim amounts
- Risk data: Property details, vehicle information, health declarations (where applicable)
- Usage data: Portal login history, communication preferences

## Data Retention

- Active policy data: Retained for the duration of the policy plus 7 years
- Claims data: Retained for 7 years after claim settlement
- Marketing data: Retained until consent is withdrawn
- Portal activity logs: Retained for 2 years

## Your Rights

Under GDPR Articles 15-22, you have the right to: access your data, rectify inaccuracies, request erasure (subject to legal retention requirements), restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing.
