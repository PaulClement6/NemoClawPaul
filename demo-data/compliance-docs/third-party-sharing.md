# Meridian Insurance Group — Third-Party Data Sharing Policy

## Parties We Share Data With

### Reinsurers
We share policy and claims data with our reinsurance partners to manage our risk exposure. Data shared includes policy type, sum insured, claims history, and risk assessment scores. **We do not share your name or contact details with reinsurers** — data is pseudonymised using policy reference numbers.

### Claims Handlers and Loss Adjusters
When you make a claim, we may share relevant policy and incident details with appointed loss adjusters, surveyors, or specialist claims handlers. These parties act as our data processors under written agreements.

### Fraud Prevention Services
We participate in industry fraud prevention schemes including the Claims and Underwriting Exchange (CUE) and the Insurance Fraud Bureau (IFB). We share claims data to detect and prevent fraudulent claims. This is a legal obligation under insurance regulation.

### Regulatory Bodies
We share data with the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA), and Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) as required by law.

### Credit Reference Agencies
For premium payment plans, we may share data with credit reference agencies to assess creditworthiness. We will inform you before doing so.

## International Transfers

We do not routinely transfer personal data outside the UK/EEA. Where transfers are necessary (e.g., for global reinsurance placements), we use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the ICO.

## Your Right to Object

You may object to data sharing for direct marketing purposes at any time. You cannot object to data sharing required by law or necessary for your insurance contract.
